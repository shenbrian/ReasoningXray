⭐ Phase 13 Semantic Lock — “Reasoning Direction”
🎯 Purpose (Patient Reality)
A patient sitting after consultation is not asking:
What is the reasoning stage?
What is the diagnostic horizon?
What is the momentum classification?
They are asking:
“Are we getting closer to knowing what’s going on?”
This signal must answer that — clearly, calmly, truthfully.
✅ Final Direction Categories (Locked)
We deliberately keep only four states.
Anything more becomes doctor-thinking, not patient-thinking.
1️⃣ Moving Toward Clearer Understanding
Patient Meaning
Doctor’s thinking is becoming more focused
Fewer possibilities
More specific plan
More confidence emerging
Emotional Effect
reassurance
forward motion
sense of progress
Typical Clinical Situations
test results narrowing options
response to treatment clarifying cause
pattern becoming recognisable
differential list shrinking
2️⃣ Largely Unchanged
Patient Meaning
No major shift since last visit
Monitoring phase
Waiting for time / response / evidence
Emotional Effect
stabilising expectation
prevents false urgency
prevents false optimism
Typical Clinical Situations
watch-and-review plan
symptoms stable
awaiting investigations
no new decisive information
3️⃣ Exploring New Possibilities
Patient Meaning
Doctor has widened thinking
New angle or hypothesis
New test / referral / approach
Emotional Effect
can feel unsettling
but framed as active problem-solving
Typical Clinical Situations
symptoms not fitting prior assumption
treatment not working
new symptom appears
second opinion or escalation
4️⃣ Becoming More Uncertain
Patient Meaning
Situation is less clear than before
Prior assumptions weakened
More ambiguity recognised
Emotional Effect
must be handled very carefully
honesty + containment
Typical Clinical Situations
conflicting results
fluctuating clinical picture
unexpected lack of response
diagnostic dead-end
🧠 Critical Tone Doctrine
This is extremely important.
The signal must NEVER:
sound like diagnostic failure
sound like doctor incompetence
imply danger unless real
use technical uncertainty language
imply “we don’t know what we are doing”
It must ALWAYS:
frame movement as part of careful care
show that thinking is active
show that uncertainty is recognised (not ignored)
maintain trust continuity
🧩 Explanation Structure (Locked)
Each direction explanation should follow this pattern:
What has changed
→ What this means
→ What usually happens next
Example pattern:
“Since the last visit, the doctor’s thinking has become more focused.
This usually means some possibilities are becoming less likely while others are being examined more closely.
Further monitoring or targeted tests may help confirm the cause.”
This gives:
narrative continuity
expectation containment
consultation memory anchor
⚖️ Mapping From Existing Signals (No New Data)
We derive direction from:
ReasoningMovement
ReasoningStage
TurningPoint presence
CertaintyTrend
Evidence dependence tone
No persistence.
No new enums required externally.
Pure interpretive synthesis.
🚨 Guardrail Rule (Very Important)
If signals are weak or contradictory → default to:
Largely Unchanged
Never force artificial movement classification.
This preserves:
clinical honesty
system credibility
patient trust
🧭 Why This Phase Is Correct Now
Because currently the system tells patients:
what happened
how thinking looks
But not yet:
“What direction the story is moving.”
This small addition:
reduces cognitive ambiguity
increases perceived competence
increases emotional containment
improves longitudinal meaning
All without:
architectural movement
feature expansion
technical risk
